Privacy policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you use ImmigratePath, we collect information you provide directly to us (such as name, contact details, and immigration case details), information needed to prepare your application (supporting documents, employment history, family information), and technical information about how you use our service.

How we use your information

  • To prepare and submit your immigration application
  • To connect you with a licensed attorney for legal review
  • To provide case updates, deadline reminders, and support
  • To improve our service and develop new features

Who we share it with

We share your information only with the licensed attorney assigned to your case and with service providers who help us operate the platform (such as secure document storage and payment processing). We do not sell your data. We do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How we protect it

All documents and personal information are encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is strictly limited to the attorney assigned to your case and authorized personnel supporting your application. We maintain SOC 2 compliance standards for data handling.

Your rights

You can request a copy of your data, correct inaccurate information, or ask us to delete your account at any time. Contact us at privacy@immigratepath.com to exercise these rights.

Questions

If you have questions about this privacy policy, email us at privacy@immigratepath.com.

Terms of service

Last updated: April 2026

What ImmigratePath is

ImmigratePath is a document preparation and case management platform. We help you organize, prepare, and track your immigration application. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice directly.

Attorney services

All legal services on our platform are provided by licensed immigration attorneys operating independently. When you purchase a case package, you enter into an attorney-client relationship with the licensed attorney assigned to review and file your application, not with ImmigratePath. The attorney is solely responsible for all legal advice, strategy, and decisions related to your case.

Fees and refunds

Our flat fees cover platform access, AI-powered intake, document preparation support, and attorney review and filing. Government filing fees charged by USCIS are separate and paid directly to the government. If you cancel before your attorney begins review, you are eligible for a full refund. Once attorney review has begun, fees are non-refundable.

No outcome guarantee

Immigration outcomes depend on many factors outside our control, including USCIS processing times, policy changes, and the accuracy of information you provide. We cannot guarantee approval of your application. We can guarantee that your attorney will prepare and file your application with professional care.

Your responsibilities

You agree to provide accurate and complete information throughout your case. Providing false information on an immigration application is a federal crime with serious consequences including denial, deportation, and criminal prosecution.

Limitation of liability

ImmigratePath is not liable for the legal advice or professional conduct of independent attorneys using the platform. Attorneys carry their own professional liability insurance. Our liability for platform-related issues is limited to the fees you paid us.

Changes to these terms

We may update these terms from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email. Continued use of the platform after changes means you accept the updated terms.
